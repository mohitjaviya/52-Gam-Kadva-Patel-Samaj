Our Story

How It All Began

It started with a simple thought: "What if our village had a digital directory?" Two friends — Mohit Javiya and Bhargesh Vamja — were the first to dream it. They imagined something small and local, just for their home village of Varsada. But as they talked more and saw the bigger picture, the vision grew into something far greater — a platform to connect every family across all 52 Gam Kadva Patel Samaj.

"We didn't just build a website — we built a bridge. A bridge connecting students, professionals, and businesses across 52 villages who share the same roots, the same values, and the same community spirit."

The journey wasn't without challenges. Building a platform from scratch brought many technical and logistical obstacles. But this team of four faced every hurdle with calm, maturity, and a shared purpose. Every feature you see today was built with care, curiosity, and genuine love for the community it serves.
