Community Directory — User Guide

Connecting 78 villages across 9 talukas
Students • Professionals • Business Owners

📌 What is This Software?

This is a Community Directory web application for the 52 Gam Kadva Patel Samaj. It connects community members from 78 villages across multiple talukas — Amreli, Babra, Lathi, Liliya, Kunkavav, Bhavnagar, Jasdan, Gondal, and Junagadh.

Members can register their profile with personal and professional details, making it easy for the community to find students, working professionals, and business owners from their villages.

📋 How to Use — Step by Step Guide

1 Sign Up (Create Account)

  1. Go to the website and click "Sign Up"
  2. Enter your phone number and email address
  3. Create a password (minimum 6 characters)
  4. You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on your email
  5. Enter the 6-digit OTP to verify your account
  6. ✅ Account created! You will be redirected to complete your profile

2 Complete Registration (Fill Your Profile)

After sign up, you need to fill your profile in 3 simple steps:

Step 2.1 — Personal Information

  • Select your Village from the dropdown (78 villages available)
  • Enter your First Name, Middle Name, Last Name
  • Select Gender (Male / Female / Other)
  • Enter your Current Address

Step 2.2 — Occupation Details

Choose one of three categories:

Step 2.3 — Review & Submit

  • Review all your details
  • Click "Submit" to send your profile for admin approval
  • ✅ Your profile is submitted!

3 Wait for Admin Approval

  • After submitting your registration, the admin will review your profile
  • You will receive an email notification when your profile is approved
  • ⏳ Until approved, your profile will not appear in the search directory

4 Login (After Approval)

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Enter your Email and Password
  3. You may need to verify with an OTP sent to your email
  4. ✅ Once logged in, you can view your profile and search the directory
💡 Forgot Password? Click "Forgot Password" on the login page → Enter your email → Receive OTP → Set a new password.

5 Search the Directory

This is the main feature! You can search for community members using filters:

  1. Go to "Search Directory" from the navigation menu
  2. Use the filter options:
FilterWhat it does
VillageSelect a specific village to see only that village's members
OccupationChoose Student, Working Professional, or Business Owner
NameSearch by first, middle, or last name

When you select an occupation, extra filters appear:

  • Student: College/University, Course/Department, Specialization
  • Working Professional: Company Name, Job Field
  • Business Owner: Business Type

Click "Search" to get results. Click on any member card to view full details.

🔑 Important: All filters work together! If you select a village AND occupation, only members matching BOTH will appear.

6 View & Edit Your Profile

  1. Click "My Profile" in the navigation
  2. View all your registered details
  3. Click "Edit Profile" to update your information
  4. You can also change your password from the profile page
  5. You can change your occupation type if it changes (e.g., student → job)

  • Count of students, working professionals, business owners
  • Village-wise distribution chart

🔒 Privacy & Security

  • Passwords are encrypted (bcrypt hashing)
  • Email verification via OTP required for login
  • Female members' phone and email are hidden from public search
  • Every registration requires admin approval before going live
  • Sensitive data access is controlled by admin
